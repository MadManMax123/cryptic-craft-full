OFFICIAL RULES

All participants must comply

EVENT: ACTIVE DURING YUVAKARSHAN 2K26

⚠ Violation of rules may result in disqualification or ban
💬 Discord Rules ▼
›Do not share flags, solutions, or direct answers with other participant teams. Each team should solve challenges independently unless the challenge explicitly allows collaboration.
›Do not run automated scripts, brute-force tools, or bots against the challenges unless the challenge explicitly requires or permits it. You may acquire the flag by unfair means but every challenge has been designed such that brute forcing your way through a level will prevent you from progressing.
›Do not attempt to attack, exploit, or disrupt the server hosting the challenges. Only interact with the intended challenge interfaces. If you notice anything wrong, please inform the @Editor 💳.
›Only target files, services, and endpoints that are clearly part of the challenge. Anything outside that scope is strictly off-limits.
›Do not post hints, decoded messages, or partial solutions in public channels. Use designated hint or help channels if you need assistance.
›Do not repost challenge files, flags, or solutions outside the server while the event is active.
›Solve challenges using cryptography and reasoning, not by exploiting unintended vulnerabilities in the platform or hosting environment.
›Be respectful in discussions. No harassment, toxicity, or belittling other participants.
›Do not create multiple accounts to bypass limits, farm hints, or gain unfair advantages.
›Moderators have the final say in rule interpretation. If they ask you to stop a behavior or change something, comply immediately.
›Each team can any number of reps in the server and upto 3 reps in the platform.
›The @Editor 💳 will let you know if there are any bugs and any important information.
›DMing admins personally is not allowed.
🖥️ Platform Rules ▼
›Each team will be given a seperate platform ID to use in the official challenge website.
›Do NOT share your team ID no matter what. It may be prone to sabotage.
›You may use the terminal view for a more classy look and feel.
›You may add a maximum of 3 reps per team in the Edit team menu.
›DO NOT reveal your flag in the server. Simply submit in the platform. You will recieve the requisite points.
›Every level gives you 2 or 3 points. A hint costs 3 or 4 points. You have 3 hints. Use them well.
›Admins will answer all questions asked in the platform within 12 hours.
›Hint reversal is NOT permitted. Once points have been spent for hints, you will not be allowed to regain the points.
›Use the team notepad to maintain notes.
›If you notice a bug, report to @Admin 👑 immidiately.
⛏️ Minecraft Rules ▼
›Node 6 is a Minecraft parkour challenge.
›To maintain the host server integrity, we will only allow 15 players to join at a time.
›One team may have more than 1 player.
›Players must reveal their identity after joining.
›You may use official Minecraft OR TLauncher version to launch the game.
›Using modpacks in the server is banned and strictly forbidden.
›PvP is allowed- but there are bigger threats in the server.
›Players, once banned cannot join again.
›@Minecraft maintenance will continuously monitor the server and user performance.
›Items for PvP and monster combat have been provided around the map. Food and beds have been provided at every checkpoint.
›If a particular chest or checkpoint has no items left, ping a @Minecraft maintenance to have it refilled.
›Please only take as much of each item as much you need.
›If you see something wrong with the server or if the server becomes unplayable, contact the @Editor 💳.
›Kindly message your username to an @Editor 💳 soon after joining to have it added to the whitelist.
›Login with /register <password> <password> after joining first time.
📡 Leads Rules ▼
›We request everyone to keep their leads questions WITHIN their respective private group chats.
›Leads will ONLY be answered with a "YES" or a "NO".
›45 leads per school per day.
›Leads are NOT hints. Hints are basically answers. They will have to be asked in the official platform.
›To ask a lead please kindly ping an @Admin 👑. Do not spam ping.
›Admins will NOT answer in sentences, or numbers or even one word.
›An admin may choose not to answer a question at all if it reveals too much.
›After every lead, admins will run !lead [Team ID] [Description].
›Hints are only available to the official three members.
›Leads are available from 8 a.m. to midnight.
›Fast responses (~10 minutes) every 3 hours. Otherwise within 30 minutes.
›No guaranteed responses from midnight to 8 a.m.
🏅 Membership Rules ▼
›Schools may have any number of members in the Discord server.
›Only the official representatives will be awarded certificates.
›The original trio of representatives will NOT be changed.
›If a member is banned, they will still receive participation certificates.
›Users will only be allowed into private group chats after verification using ID proof.
›If all members are banned, the team may continue but cannot use Discord benefits like leads.
›Only 3 official members allowed on the challenge platform.
